Answers to Your Burning Questions

Find the answers to common inquiries about Averti Designer and our services

Do you design for Desktop and mobile?

Yes, absolutely! We understand the importance of designing websites that deliver a seamless and engaging experience across all devices. Our team is proficient in designing for both desktop and mobile platforms to ensure your website looks amazing and functions flawlessly on any screen size.

How does payment works?

To start the project, we require a 50% deposit. After the designs are finalized, the remaining 50% must be paid in order to receive the design files.

Do you offer development service?

Yes! We are certified Webflow experts and react developers can build your design if needed. Just let us know if you need that service and we can quote you based on the final design

What do I need to get started

During the kickoff call we will tell you everything we need to get started. Typically this will include: Site overview with the sections you need, inspiration from websites you love, logo/brand guide, and any assets you'd like to use.

How long does project takes?

Most projects can be completed in 2 weeks or less depending on the speed and quality of your feedback. We offer an expedited package that delivers in 3 days.

How many revisions do I get?

Our goal is to provide you with an amazing final product, and we do not limit the number of revisions. Typically, we complete most projects within 1-2 revision cycles.
